What is it?

Kbuddy is a rails application, design as a mobile application, to help people with cancer in their everyday lives. User can add to the app their medical records & meetings or the medicine they have to take and keep a track on everything. I really appreciated working on an app that has a real social impact. I'm convinced technology should be used as a force of good. We were a team of 4 people from different backgrounds, collaborating on Github.
